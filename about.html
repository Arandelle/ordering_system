ABOUT

Inspired by the kuya who runs the grill, where food brings the barkada and family closer.

Harrison proudly serves the flavored grills in Makati from grilled, comforting dishes, and homecook favorites.

From the smoky aroma of the grill to the warm smiles that cheers you, we aim to bring the true spirit of Filipino dishes.

Whether you’re here for a quick meal or a shared feast with family and friends, we’re excited to serve you good food and good memories.

Every bite tells a story - from barkada nights to family Sundays, Harrison serves proudly Filipino inasal and BBQ.

kada kagat,

From our first flame in Harrison to every table we reach,
Harrison House of Inasal & BBQ serves stories you can taste.
